Actor prompt

Done

  1. Introduces themselves to patient and gains consent


  2. Elicits a short history of the problem (when the diagnosis was made, what were the presenting features, how has the disease progressed, has there been treatment)


  3. Ask about other symptoms


  4. Ask about difficulties with ADLs: (washing, dressing, bathing, stairs, telephone, opening doors, cooking, shopping)


  5. Elicits the nature of his work


  6. Clarifies the impact of functional limitation on his work


  7. Enquires about his financial situation


  8. Explores family and social support


  9. Summarizes accurately
