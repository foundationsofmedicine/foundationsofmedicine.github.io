Patient Task
Mr Jack Henderson, 63 yrs.

Crushing central chest pain for the last 20 minutes that isn’t going away. The pain is also in his jaw. He rates it 7/10.

Came on suddenly when he was gardening with his wife in the backyard.

The pain is worse when he tries to walk, and nothing is relieving it.

He is feeling ill, and can’t stop sweating.

Hasn’t been able to catch his breath since the pain started- breathing faster than usual.

Past Medical History
Has been experiencing mild chest discomfort and shortness of breath when walking the dog each morning (about 800m, slightly uphill) for the past few weeks. The symptoms only last for a few mins if he rests. Had put it down to getting old.

Sometimes experiences a burning pain in his legs when he walks. It goes away when he has a rest. This has been occurring for about 6 months.

He has never had any heart problems. Does not suffer from indigestion.

Has experienced chest pain once before 2 years ago, after eating. Had a metallic taste in his mouth. Attended ER but was diagnosed with GORD. Never occurred again.

Does not have diabetes, but his local GP did mention that his blood pressure and cholesterol were both high 3 years ago. Dr told him he was overweight. He hasn’t seen a Dr since then because he doesn’t like them much.

Doesn’t;t exercise regularly- walks to the shops occasionally.

Has been hospitalised twice: 1) appendix out at age 12, 2) GORD episode at age 61.

No other medical problems, except slight arthritis in his right knee.

No allergies.

Medications
No regular medications- doctor gave him a few little white tablets and a yellow one a few years back but he didn’t think he needed them so he threw them out.

Social History
He is a retired bus driver.

Smokes a pack of cigarettes (50’s) a day- has done so for 48 years.

Drinks 2 glasses of whisky on Sundays at his local RSL.

Lives in a single story home in Revesby with his wife. They have no children.

His wife does all the cooking and cleaning for him, and they are both coping well at home.

Family History
Father died of a heart attack at age 71. His mother died of a stroke at age 63.

Has an older brother (age 69) who has diabetes.

Has a younger sister (age 62) who has angina and some cholesterol problems.
