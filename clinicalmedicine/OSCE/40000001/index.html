OSCE ID: 40000001

Examination Criteria

Examinee prompt

Done

Actor prompt

Done

  1. Washes hands, introduces self, gains consent and appropriately exposes/positions patient (must have all).


  2. Tests Biceps reflex (C5-6) left and right.


  3. Tests Brachioradialis reflex (C5-6) left and right.


  4. Tests Triceps reflex (C7-8) left and right.


  5. Tests Patellar reflex (L3-4) left and right.


  6. Tests Achilles reflex (S1-2) left and right.


  7. Grades reflex response and symmetry for all of the above.


  8. Uses reinforcement manoeuvres appropriately.


  9. Tests for clonus at the knees and ankles.


  10. Tests Babinski responses.


  11. Synthesises and summarises examination findings approriately.
