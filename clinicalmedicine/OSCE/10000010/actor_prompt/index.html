Actor

Unless you are a willing volunteer, your job is to act as a co-examiner by asking the following questions:

What are the likely causes of a non-tender breast lump? (A: Cyst, carcinoma, fibroadenosis/chronic mastitits, fibroadenoma(benign) )

What other investigations could you perform? (A: Mammography, Ultrasound, FNA biopsy, Core biopsy)
