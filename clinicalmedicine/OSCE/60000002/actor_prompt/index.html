OSCE Exam ID: 60000002

Actor

You have recently been diagnosed with asthma and have come in to have spirometry performed for the first time.

On attempting spirometry, you have some difficulties the first few times:
* Not a full amount of effort on expiration (stop quickly).

* On the second attempt, slouch and allow a leak at the mouthpiece.

* On the third attempt, cough or ask if you are doing it right during the recording.

* Perform it correctly from then on, and give your full effort.
