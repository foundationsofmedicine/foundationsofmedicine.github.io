OSCE Exam ID: 50000005

Actor

Patient Task
You will need to play the part of two people for this task: Anika Smith who is a 17-year-old female who has just had a seizure, and her mother Sharon Smith who was the main witness.

Anika
Felt unusual all of a sudden while dancing with her cousins and then blacked out.

She can’t remember much after that.

Has a bad headache, a sore tongue, and her muscles feel very tired and weak.

She had eaten and had plenty to drink prior to the blackout.

Sharon
Was watching her daughter dancing in the black and white flashing strobe light.

She all of a sudden stopped and went stiff and then fell hard to the floor.

Her whole body started jerking for over a minute and she couldn’t be woken.

She wet her pants during the attack and was very disorientated when she was waking up.

She believes that her daughter is not yet back to her usual self.

Past Medical History
Has never had a seizure before.

Has fainted in the past, but this time was very different.

Anika suffers from exercise-induced asthma, but has it well controlled.

She is very healthy otherwise.

Never been hospitalised, no surgery, and no known allergies.

Medications
Takes Ventolin before she plays any sports, and if she feels tight afterwards.

Has a multivitamin ever day to try to be healthy.

Social History
Anika is a year 12 student, who is feeling anxious about her upcoming HSC exams.

She is a high-achiever, and wishes to pursue a career in law.

She is a non-smoker, and never drinks alcohol. Never taken recreational drugs.

Lives at home with her mum, dad and younger brother Trent (15 years) in Epping.

She enjoys playing softball on weekends for her local club, and has a large group of friends who she socialises with regularly.

Family History
Her maternal uncle has grand mal epilepsy. He was diagnosed at age 6.

All her immediate family members are well.

There is no other significant family history.
