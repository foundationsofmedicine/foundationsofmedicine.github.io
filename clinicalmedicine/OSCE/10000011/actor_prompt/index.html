Actor

You are a patient at this suburban medical centre.

You can make up your drug list, or talk about your own medications usage if you wish.

The assessment focuses on the student's ability to ask the right answers rather than what you have to say.

However, some good drugs you might want to mention include:

(1) Ibuprofen/celecoxib - you have found that you occasionally get some discomfort after eating food (?peptic ulcers)

(2) You use St John's Wort sometimes when you feel a bit low (i.e. when you're feeling sad)

(3) You sometimes try chinese herbal medicines when your family/friend brings some over to see you

(4) You use hydrocortisone when you have an eczema flare up, but you don't use it often
